About me

Welcome to the website of my portfolio, which I am constantly updating. My name is Michal Závodský and I am a front-end web developer. I graduated from the University of St. Cyril and Methodius in Trnava, Department of Mass Media Communication and Marketing. Since I was more interested in website development I found a job in this field. I have been working as a front-end developer for about a year and a half now. During this time I worked on several projects, the main ones are listed above. I have experience working with HTML, CSS / SCSS and jQuery. I have also worked on several Wordpress websites so I have experience with Wordpress and very basic PHP. I also have experience with creating responsive websites and SEO. I also more or less speak English. I am currently learning React and looking for a job as a front-end web developer at the junior level - what I don't know, I'll be happy to learn :).
