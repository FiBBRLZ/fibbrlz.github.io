About me

Welcome to the website of my portfolio. My name is Michal Závodský and I am a front-end web developer. I am also an avid cyclist and runner. When I am not writing code you can find me somewhere outside on one of my bicycles. :)

I graduated from the University of St. Cyril and Methodius in Trnava, Department of Mass Media Communication and Marketing. However I was more interested in web development which led me to a job in this field. I have been working as a front-end developer for about 6 years now. Last 4 years I spent in wonderful company called Grandaliro which helped me grow my skillsets and as a person.

During this time I worked on various projects, the latest or interesting ones are listed above. I have experience working with HTML, CSS / SCSS / Tailwind and jQuery. I have also worked with CMS such as Worpdress and Drupal. I have experience with creating responsive layouts / design and SEO. I also know English at an intermediate level. I mainly read, so my spoken English is not that good but with practise I can get back in to it.

I would like to find my next job as a React front-end web developer. At this time I have only worked on a few projects that used Next.js and Tailwind - what I don't know(and that's a lot), I'll be happy to learn :).
